# 📌 Project Overview

SmartInventory AI is an end-to-end inventory intelligence platform for
supermarkets, bakeries, restaurants, retail chains, warehouses, and
manufacturing industries. It leverages Artificial Intelligence, Machine
Learning, SQL analytics, Excel dashboards, Power BI, and Streamlit to
predict expiry risks, optimize inventory, forecast demand, and recover
revenue before losses occur.

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Businesses lose significant revenue due to expired products,
overstocking, poor demand forecasting, and delayed inventory decisions.
Existing inventory systems track stock but rarely provide predictive
insights or AI-driven recommendations.

# ❌ Why Existing Systems Fail

-   Reactive instead of proactive
-   No expiry prediction
-   No demand forecasting
-   No revenue recovery strategy
-   Limited business intelligence

# 💡 Proposed Solution

-   Predict expiry 14 days in advance
-   Forecast demand
-   Calculate revenue at risk
-   Recommend dynamic discounts
-   Suggest store-to-store inventory transfer
-   Optimize production planning
-   Provide interactive dashboards
